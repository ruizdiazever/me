Ever Ruiz Diaz

Hello! I am Ever. I became a web developer because i love solving complex problems and I want to make a difference.

I am currently based in Rome but i was born and bred in Buenos Aires.

Although my professional path has taken twists and turns, I have always nurtured my deep passion in computer science. I become a self-taught HTML5, CSS3 and Bootstrap and Java mobile programming in Android at the age of 24.

Soon after, I shifted my interests to web development and I met PYTHON. This is how my never ending love with DJANGO started.

I have done work on all sorts of web projects ranging from software development and mobile app creation to front-end/back-end web design and development and database/server management.

Curious by nature and eager to develop new skills, I always find the time to keep myself up-to-date with the latest technologies. I am currently studying DOCKER and increasing my expertise on JavaSript.

In 2017, I participated to the competition “Plan your Future” (“Programa tu futuro”) sponsored by the governmental of Buenos Aires. My project, a mobile app to promote the cultural event of the city, won the 2nd prize.

My other passions are my family, photography and the fight against climate change. I started my own personal photography blog, Light, in 2019, to share the world through my eyes.
